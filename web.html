DATA WIZARDS


The DATA WIZARDS was an immersive experience where participants not only explored the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence and Large Language Models (LLMs), but also showcased their prompt creation finesse. Neural Quest is a thrilling challenge where participants put their Artificial Intelligence skills to the test in a high-stakes, cash-prize hunt. From crafting razor-sharp prompts to solving real-time AI challenges, contestants must harness the power of LLMs, RAG, and creative problem-solving to outsmart the competition and claim the reward.
